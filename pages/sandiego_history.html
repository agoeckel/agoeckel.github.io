San Diego History

  • The recorded history of the San Diego, California, region began in the present state of California when San Diego Bay was first discovered by Europeans.

  • Native Americans had been living in the area for as long as 12,000 years.

  • San Diego became part of the U.S. in 1848.

  • Entrepreneurs and boosters laid the basis for an economy based today on the military, defense industries, tourism, international trade, and manufacturing.

  • San Diego is now the eighth largest city in the country and forms the heart of the larger San Diego metropolitan area.
